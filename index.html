How WHS Handicap is Calculated
Each round produces a Score Differential = (Adjusted Gross Score − Course Rating) × 113 ÷ Slope Rating.
With 3–19 rounds, the best differentials are averaged (1 of 3, 1 of 4, 2 of 5–6, etc.).
With 20+ rounds, the best 8 of your last 20 differentials are averaged × 0.96.
Maximum handicap index is 54.0.
